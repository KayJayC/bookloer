Ekta Sunday
The Time Machine" by H.G Wells
It is one of the best sci-fi thriller story especially if you love to read science fiction stories. The plot centers on a time machine that can go into the past or the future. In this story, the author ventures into the future and reveals many things that he observed there. He also observed that, after millions of years, there was no longer any life on Earth. If you continue reading, you will be quite interested to learn what will happen next in this tale. This narrative does not have a satisfactory conclusion, but the middle portion of the book is fantastic and full of original ideas.

Omo Tuesday
Under The Whispering Door by TJ Klune
In terms of pacing, the book is mostly a quick and easy read. I found the first half a tiny bit slow and occasionally aimless, but I think the book picks up a lot in the second half and has a much clearer sense of direction, especially heading into the end. It helps a lot that the book is often genuinely funny, which helped me get through the relatively slower parts of the book. I also enjoyed the vibrant cast of characters, and the general vibe of it is that it’s a cute and whimsical story. I appreciate that it’s heart-warming and entertaining. I’m very specifically using the word relatively here, since this is a fairly quick read overall.

Suspense
Cozy
Zoe Wednesday
A Darker Shade of Magic by V.E. Schwab
A Darker Shade of Magic by V.E. Schwab has a unique and fascinating world and magic system that wraps you up and keeps you waiting with baited breath for what comes next. It's fast-paced with a pair of fun and witty protagonists and a terrifying antagonist. It's absolutely a must-read for fantasy enthusiasts.
